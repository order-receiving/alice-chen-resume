fractured

The Fractured is the subject of my art work. I use the emotional fragmentation of people and the physical fragmentation of sculptures to express my feelings. In addition, I combine stitch, collage, crayon dot press and other ways to enrich the layers. What I want to express is that now, when people's emotions are fragmented, they still need to pick themselves up one by one and stitch them together in front of others.

kill that pitaya

Pitaya is a fruit I hate. I use this inspiration to express my emotions by trampling and squeezing. At the same time, I try different textures and materials, such as scraper, plastic foam, 3D printing etc., to convey different emotions. The blood-like texture is the SAP of Pitaya, which also symbolizes fear and blood. Violent techniques represent catharsis.
